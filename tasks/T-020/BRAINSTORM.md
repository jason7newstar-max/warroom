# T-020 · Spec-Makeover outreach engine (micro-brands <10K followers)

**Spark:** Chairman 2026-06-06 — the sharpest, scaled version of the T-018 cold-pitch. Essentially the **ONE TEN COMPANY core motion** (AI-fast cinematic content + sites for SMBs, niche by niche).

## The play
Target TINY brands (**<10K IG followers**, just-starting / early-traction). For each:
1. Pull ~6 of their everyday IG photos.
2. Generate **cinematic versions** of their own content (their product elevated — keep product ACCURATE, like T-014).
3. Build them a **micro-site** (like the energy site).
4. Package as a private "**we already made this for you**" demo → DM/email → with a **payment plan** to keep it.
5. Convert. The finished makeover IS the pitch (irresistible — it's THEIR brand, transformed).

## Why it's strong
- <10K brands = founder-run, no agency, hungry, cash-light (payment plan unlocks them), reachable.
- Hook beats everyone: not "hire us" but "here's your brand looking world-class — want it?"
- Scalable with his AI tools + the war room. The makeover = the conversion artifact.

## ⚠️ IP / copyright (Chairman asked) — LOW risk if done as a PRIVATE pitch
- Using a brand's OWN photos to make a demo FOR them, sent privately = transformative + for their benefit + not published/commercialized → low risk.
- Rules: **keep PRIVATE** (don't post makeovers publicly until they're a paying client); **avoid photos clearly shot by a 3rd-party photographer** (brand may not own those); **sort content rights on conversion**; keep product depiction accurate.

## ⚠️ Other flags
- **Don't mass-blast.** Mass IG scrape/DM trips Instagram anti-automation (bans) AND reads spammy → hurts the ONE TEN brand. Personalized, quality, small batches.
- **Economics:** conversion rate on the demo × payment-plan price = viability. Prove on ONE before scaling.
