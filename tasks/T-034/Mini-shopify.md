# Mini — Shopify take

Honest answer: generic Shopify dropship/resell with no inventory is usually not a good 2026 bet. It can profit only if you are strong at product picks, ads, creatives, supplier QA, returns, and support. For $1000 in / $0 revenue, ad spend can vanish before fit.

Vs ShotPro: software is harder to build but has better economics: owned IP, near-zero marginal cost, higher margin, compounding product value. Shopify resell is low-moat ops.

Vs Amazon affiliate: affiliate has lower upside but far lower risk: no inventory, support, refunds, or chargebacks. Better as a traffic test.

NYC cheap, good local goods: feasible only as a curated local brand with real sourcing/QA/story/fast delivery. Not passive dropship.

Verdict: skip generic dropship now. Prioritize ShotPro + affiliate traffic; revisit NYC goods after demand is proven.
