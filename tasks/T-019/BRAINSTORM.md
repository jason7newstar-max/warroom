# T-019 · DJ Agency — NYC EDM/club bookings (idea)

**Spark:** Chairman 2026-06-06. He's in NYC, has a DJ/electronic background (PINLA/Moon1k), ran a DJ studio. Idea: a DJ booking **agency** = middleman connecting DJs (China/Korea/Japan/US) → NYC nightclubs / EDM festivals / electronic clubs (NOT wedding DJs). Get DJ sets → build profiles → pitch NYC venues.

## Real assets / edge
- NYC + insider to the electronic scene (credibility — he IS electronic).
- **Asia (CN/KR/JP) DJ bridge** = genuine differentiator (language/culture middleman; underserved niche).
- **Content machine (ONE TEN STUDIO)** = the killer wedge: cinematic DJ profiles/reels/EPKs, fast+cheap. Most agencies' DJ promo is weak. (Same "content = the pitch" insight as T-018.)

## Hard truths (DJ booking is one of the hardest businesses)
1. **Demand side is the killer** — getting NYC clubs to actually PAY to book your (unknown, Asian) DJs. Venues book on DRAW (does the DJ pull a crowd?) + trust + existing promoter/resident relationships. Cold-starting a new agency here is brutal.
2. **Visas** — CN/KR/JP DJs performing in the US need P/O performer visas → legal cost + lead time. Real barrier.
3. **Cash flow / liability** — deposits, contracts, riders, travel, no-shows; the middleman carries it.
4. **Two-sided chicken-and-egg** — need DJs to land venues, venues to sign DJs.

## De-risked entry (don't start as a generalist agency — you'll drown)
- **Content-first:** start as a cinematic DJ **EPK/promo service** (uses his strength, low risk, builds roster + portfolio) → graduate to booking once there's a roster + venue relationships.
- **Niche hard on Asia→NYC** electronic talent — be THE bridge for that flavor, not "all DJs."
- **Or throw your own events** (promote a night at a club with your DJs) — higher risk, full control, proves the model, builds both sides at once.

## Verdict
Cooler-than-it-is-easy. The content + Asia-bridge are real wedges; pure booking is hard/slow/heavy. **Pursue only via the content-first or niche-event entry**, not as a generic agency. If pursued: war-room research needed on NYC venue booking mechanics, P/O visa reality, existing DJ-agency models, Asian-DJ supply. **Lower priority / backlog unless Chairman wants to push.**
