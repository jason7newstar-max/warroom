# T-030 ShotPro Distribution Wedge

Mini recommendation:

Single move: build a free public Before/After Makeover page, not Shopify first. Shopify apps need marketplace trust/review time; a public generator gives instant share loops + cold-outreach bait.

This week:
Day 1: landing page `/free` with 1 upload, choose 6 styles, email capture, usage consent checkbox.
Day 2: generate 1 free style + 5 locked previews; output has subtle ShotPro watermark + "made from one phone photo" footer.
Day 3: share page per result with OG image, copy link, download watermarked.
Day 4: paid unlock: remove watermark + get all 6 styles + batch credits at $9.99/mo.
Day 5: seed 200 micro Shopify/Etsy sellers: DM with their current product photo + custom makeover link.
Day 6-7: add referral code: "remove watermark free if 3 seller friends try it."

Metric: upload->email, email->paid, share->upload. Wedge = sellers distribute the proof image because it flatters their own product.
