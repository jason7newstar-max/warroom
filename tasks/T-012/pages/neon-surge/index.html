Product Story

Clean lift for heat, lights, bass, and motion.

A summer festival is not a normal afternoon. It is sun exposure, miles of walking, a wall of sound, and a schedule that peaks after midnight. Neon Surge frames energy as a smoother ritual: crack the can before the headliner, get a bright citrus snap, then settle into alert, social momentum without the syrupy weight of old-school energy drinks.

The campaign language leans premium and functional: hydration-minded electrolytes, B-vitamins, caffeine for focus, and a dry sparkling finish that tastes adult enough for rooftop parties and clean enough for gym bags.
