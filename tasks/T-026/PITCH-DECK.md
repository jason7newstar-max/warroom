# ONE TEN LAB — Investor Pitch Deck (Outline)

*AI-accelerated creative studio/lab. Cinematic content + websites + original music for small brands — one niche at a time.*

---

## Slide 1 — Problem
**Small brands can't afford the content that makes them look big.**
- A cinematic video, a real website, and original music = 3 vendors, weeks of time, $10k+.
- Sub-10K-follower brands get ignored by agencies and out-priced by freelancers.
- DIY tools (Canva, templates) all look the same — no soul, no story.

---

## Slide 2 — Solution
**One AI-accelerated lab that ships film + site + music as a single package.**
- We compress a 3-vendor, multi-week pipeline into days, niche by niche.
- Cinematic look, custom jingle, live micro-site — accurate to the real product.
- "Made this for you" private spec demos, not generic templates.

---

## Slide 3 — Product + Live Demos
**Already built and running — not a deck-only company.**
- Studio hub: **oneten-studio.vercel.app** — portfolio of cinematic spec work.
- Cafe spec sites — full niche micro-sites built from a brand's own photos.
- Custom-jingle engine — natural language → original music score → audio.

---

## Slide 4 — Why Now
**AI just crossed the line from toy to production crew.**
- Image/video/music models are finally brand-accurate and cinematic.
- One small team can now output what took a full agency in 2022.
- Small brands are desperate for content velocity to feed IG/TikTok feeds.

---

## Slide 5 — Market
**Millions of micro-brands, each needing content forever.**
- Tens of millions of SMBs + creators under 10K followers globally.
- Content is a recurring need, not a one-time buy — built-in retention.
- Wedge niche-by-niche (cafes first), then replicate the playbook.

---

## Slide 6 — Business Model
**Free 7-day spec → low-friction subscription → expansion.**
- Free 7-day spec demo (expires) — proof before payment, near-zero CAC.
- **$9.99/mo** base: hosted micro-site + content refreshes.
- Add-ons: extra videos, custom jingles, site pages — margin on top of subs.

---

## Slide 7 — Traction So Far
**A working pipeline and real spec output, in weeks.**
- Live portfolio + multiple spec sites and product pages deployed.
- Repeatable cinematic video + jingle + site pipeline (reusable templates).
- Multi-agent "war room" produces content faster each cycle.

---

## Slide 8 — Go-To-Market
**Automated, targeted, spec-first — no mass blast.**
- Automated local-SMB outreach: scrape niche → build one real spec → private demo.
- IG DMs with a "made this for you" preview link, not a cold pitch.
- 7-day expiry creates urgency; one prototype per brand keeps it personal + low-risk.

---

## Slide 9 — Team
**A lean, AI-native creative crew that ships daily.**
- Founder-led studio with a multi-agent production system (the "war room").
- Blended human direction + AI execution across film, web, and music.
- Built and shipped live products before raising a dollar.

---

## Slide 10 — The Ask
**Raise to turn a working lab into a self-running content engine.**
- Funding to scale outreach automation + conversion across niches.
- Hire to deepen the production pipeline and hold quality at volume.
- Goal: thousands of subscribed micro-brands on the free-spec → $9.99/mo motion.
